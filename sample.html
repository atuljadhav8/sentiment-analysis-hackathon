      "Development team"

      StarStarStarStarStar
      • Work/Life Balance

      • Compensation and Benefits
      • Senior Management
      Current Employee - Anonymous Employee 
      Recommends
      Approves of CEO

      I have been working at Travelex full-time

      Pros

      Being part of software development team from past couple of years, i can say that few positive major initiatives have been taken in order to change the way how the company's Management and technology decisions are taken. I am happy that the current focus of Travelex is the Cloud Transformation which will help the company to first find out the applications which are worth to migrate on cloud or needs an upgrade.... If i can think from long term perspective like 5 years down the line, work in Travelex is the latest as compared to most of the other product companies. I have seen a boom in adapting new technologies in Travelex from last 2 year. Its very rapid move, which needs lot of appreciation to both Management and the technology teams. Focusing more on Micro and nano services aspects, which calls for a merger between Develepers, testers and support. So if anyone wants to grow as a team to work as part of whole SDLC, I will not hesitate to name Travelex. But at the same time this requires every team member to upgrade their skills to get an opportunity to work in these teams. If you are still stuck with your old skill sets as part og not doing your homework, you no where get any new opportunity, no matter which company it is. But have seen Travelex giving lot of chance to everyone irrespective whether the person deserve it or not. Team dinners and parties are also arranged. Atleast in my team, no micro management is done. Couple of inhouse and paid classroom trainings are also arranged. Its upto you to take benefit of it and implement in your work. Cab facility available Quarterly bonus and awards

      Show More

      Cons

      Have seen many times that third party technology solutions are from Vendors which are not ready to support well for issues in their systems. In such cases, Vendors should be pressurized rather than wasting money, time and effort of solving someone else errors.

      Advice to Management

      Need to be proactive and stream line the projects used rather than creating hell lot of different applications of which more than half of the company people are not aware and creating chaos.
